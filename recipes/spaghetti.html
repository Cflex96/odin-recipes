Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

Description

This is an italian classic and the perfect recipe for lazy evenings where you still don't want to miss up on a delicious dinner. As the name indicates are the main ingredients spaghetti, oil and garlic and therefore always available, even if your kitchen is filled sparse on a particular day.

Ingredients

For 1 serving

  • 150g spaghetti
  • olive oil
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 1 dried pepperoncino
  • parmesan cheese
  • parsley
  • salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Slice up the garlic and the pepperoncino into slices
  2. Cook the pasta al dente. While the pasta is off cooking:
  3. Put around 2 tablespoons of olive oil into a pan at medium heat. Add the garlic and the pepperoncino and fry them until the garlic turns brown.
  4. When the pasta is ready let it drain and add it to the pan with the garlic. You can add 2 more tablespoons of oil, salt and pepper.
  5. Grate the parmesan and chop up the parsley and add them on top when serving
