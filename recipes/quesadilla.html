Quesadillas

Description

I discovered this recipe for Quesadillas when I first left home for university. What's great about his recipe is that it is easy to make and explosive in flavour. Who said students have to eat frugally?

Ingredients

for 2 servings

  • 8 Tortillas
  • 2 cans of canned kidney beans
  • 20g of fresh cilantro
  • 1 shallot
  • cherry tomatoes
  • pickled jalapeños
  • cheddar cheese
  • cumin
  • chili flakes
  • 1 lime

Steps

  1. Chop up the cilantro and the shallot and put it in a bowl
  2. Drain the beans and add them
  3. Now add 1 teaspoon each of cumin and chili flakes
  4. Stir everything, add the limejuice and mash everything together with a spoon
  5. Cut the cherry tomatoes into fine slices and grate the cheese
  6. Now we are ready to put everything together: Take a tortilla spread some of your beanfilling on top. Just be careful not to add too much of the filling on top, as it can disturb the balance of the dish. Now genrously add the tomatoes and the jalapeños to your personal liking. Last but not least finish the Quesadilla with the greated cheese. Top it off with a second tortilla and put it in a pan at medium heat. Bake until crispy
  7. Now there is nothing left to do but enjoy it.
